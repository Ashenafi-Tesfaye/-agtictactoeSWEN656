Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe!

To start playing, click on "Start New Game". You can share the game with a friend by copying the game link or sending it via email.

How to Play: Take turns clicking on the board to place your mark. The first player to align three of their marks vertically, horizontally, or diagonally wins!

Winning the Game: Keep an eye on your opponent's moves and block them if they are close to winning. Use strategy to place your marks and win the game.
